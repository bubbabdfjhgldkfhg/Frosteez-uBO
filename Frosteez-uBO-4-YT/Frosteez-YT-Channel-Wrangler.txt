! DOM note: anchored on the stable yt-content-metadata-view-model TAG + text content, NOT the
!   volatile class names (YouTube A/B-s ytContentMetadataViewModelMetadataRow vs the kebab form).
!     views = yt-content-metadata-view-model span:has-text(/views|watching/)
!     age   = yt-content-metadata-view-model span:has-text(/ ago/)   (leading space avoids "Chicago" etc.)
!     title = h3[title]    duration = badge-shape:has-text(/:\d\d/)
!   Scoping the count/age spans to yt-content-metadata-view-model also keeps channel-name text from
!   false-matching the view-count regexes (channel name is its own row in this DOM).

! Hide by video length — read runtime from the title aria-label (STABLE). The thumbnail badge is NOT
!   usable: on hover YouTube plays a preview and the badge changes to elapsed time (1:32:36 -> 0:03),
!   which the old badge rule misread as "short" and deleted long videos. aria-label keeps the true
!   runtime. Keep >=10min = has "hour" or two-digit minutes; the :matches-attr(/minute|second/) guard
!   skips live videos (no duration); the $ anchor on minutes avoids matching "10 Minutes" in a title.
  !! Hide videos <10 minutes
  youtube.com##ytd-rich-item-renderer:has([href="/@FirstWeFeast"]):has(a[href^="/watch"][aria-label]:matches-attr(aria-label="/minute|second/"):not(:matches-attr(aria-label="/hour|[1-9]\d minutes?(, \d+ seconds?)?$/"))):remove()
  youtube.com##ytd-rich-item-renderer:has(a:matches-attr(href="/@(hudsoninstitute|FPRI|csis|cfr|BrookingsInstitution|CNASdc|ChathamHouse10|HooverInstitution|CarnegieEndowment|TheStimsonCenter|SIPRIorg)$/")):has(a[href^="/watch"][aria-label]:matches-attr(aria-label="/minute|second/"):not(:matches-attr(aria-label="/hour|[1-9]\d minutes?(, \d+ seconds?)?$/"))):remove()
